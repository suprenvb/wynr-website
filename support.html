Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Download WYNR from the App Store and tap "Sign Up" to create your account using your email address. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.
How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a link to reset your password within a few minutes.
Is WYNR free to use?
WYNR offers a free tier with access to basic features. Premium features are available through a subscription for users who want advanced analytics and additional picks.
How does WYNR analyze bets?
WYNR uses an ensemble of prediction models including Elo ratings, statistical regression, and sport-specific metrics to identify value in betting lines. Our models analyze real-time odds across major sportsbooks.
Which sportsbooks does WYNR support?
We track odds from major sportsbooks including DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, and more. WYNR helps you find the best available odds across these platforms.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account and all associated data, please email support@wynr.ai with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and we'll process your request within 48 hours.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
We appreciate bug reports! Please email support@wynr.ai with a description of the issue, your device type, and any screenshots if possible.
